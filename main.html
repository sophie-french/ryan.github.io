About Me

I took up diving just over three years ago and I absolutely love it! The feeling of floating through the water and seeing the amazing things that live under the surface is like nothing else I've ever experienced, and I'm so grateful to have found a hobby that I can enjoy for many years to come. I started diving in Thailand, which is a fantastic place to learn as the visibility is amazing and there's so much to see. I then went on to do my Advanced Open Water qualification in Australia, which was a great opportunity to see some of the more unusual marine life that you don't find in other parts of the world.

I've been lucky enough to dive in some of the most beautiful places on earth, including the Great Barrier Reef, the Maldives, and the Red Sea. I've also had some amazing experiences, such as swimming with whale sharks and manta rays.

I think that everyone should try diving at least once in their lives, as it's such a unique and exhilarating experience.

If you're thinking of taking up diving, or if you're already a certified diver, then I hope you find my blog useful and enjoyable. I'll be posting about all aspects of diving, from beginner tips to advanced techniques, and from my personal experiences to general advice. I hope you enjoy reading it!
